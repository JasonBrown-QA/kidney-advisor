Stage 3 Quick Reference

  • Stage 3a: eGFR 45–59 mL/min/1.73m² · Stage 3b: 30–44
  • BP target: typically <130/80 (confirm with your nephrologist)
  • Sodium: often <2,300 mg/day · Protein: 0.6–0.8 g/kg/day
  • Watch: NSAIDs, contrast dye, dehydration, untreated UTIs

Educational reference only — not medical advice.

CKD Diet Quick Tips

  • Lower-K swaps: apple instead of banana, white rice instead of brown, cauliflower instead of potato
  • Lower-P swaps: rice/almond milk instead of dairy; avoid cola, processed cheese, deli meat
  • Lower-Na: cook from scratch, rinse canned veg, skip table salt
  • Watch: hidden phosphate additives (any "PHOS" on the ingredient list)

Ask Advisor — Powered by Google Gemini

Get personalized CKD guidance by asking any health, diet, lifestyle, or medication question. The advisor draws from established sources (NKF, NIDDK, CDC, Mayo Clinic, KDIGO) and uses your latest labs, BP, meds, and diet data.

Privacy: Your question and the personal-context summary are sent to Google's Gemini API. Google does not use AI Studio / Gemini API data for model training on the paid tier; free-tier data may be used to improve models — check current Google terms. Your API key stays on this device.

Not medical advice. The advisor provides educational information. For decisions about treatment, medication changes, or urgent symptoms, consult your nephrologist or primary care physician — or call 911 / go to the ER for emergencies.

Export briefing (optional)

For occasional use with another chatbot (ChatGPT Enterprise, Claude, etc.), or to share with your care team: one click builds a complete briefing — the CKD advisor system prompt plus your current labs, BP, meds, and trends — that you can paste anywhere.

Set Up Your Free Gemini API Key

The Ask Advisor uses Google's Gemini API. The free tier covers ~250 questions per day — no credit card required.

  1. Go to aistudio.google.com/apikey and sign in with any Google account
  2. Click Create API key
  3. Copy the key (starts with AIza...)
  4. Paste it in the Settings tab of this app under "Ask Advisor"

Your key is stored in your browser's localStorage on this device only — not synced to OneDrive.

About

Kidney Advisor is a personal self-management tool for chronic kidney disease stage 3. It does not transmit your data anywhere — everything stays in your browser's local storage on this device, plus any sync file you opt-in to.

Not medical advice. Use alongside, not instead of, care from your nephrologist and primary physician. Always confirm targets and medication changes with your care team.
